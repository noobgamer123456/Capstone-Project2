My Favorite Hobby

One of my favorite hobbies is cycling. I enjoy riding my bicycle early in the morning or in the afternoon when the weather is calm. It gives me a sense of freedom and keeps me physically and mentally active. I love the feeling of fresh air and exploring different places around my area.

Cycling Time

Why I Love It

Cycling is not just exercise for me — it's something that helps me relax and clear my mind. It keeps me healthy and fit, and I always feel refreshed after a ride. Sometimes, I listen to music while cycling through quiet roads, and it feels peaceful and enjoyable.

Peace of Mind

Where I Go

I often go cycling around my neighborhood, local parks, and sometimes a bit further when I have more time. Riding through green areas and open roads gives me joy. I also enjoy cycling with my friends when we all have free time during holidays or weekends.

My Future Plans

I hope to take cycling more seriously in the future — maybe even join a cycling club or participate in events. It's a hobby that keeps me active, teaches me discipline, and gives me goals to chase. I want to continue it as I grow older and stay strong and healthy.
